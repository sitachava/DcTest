Nils Puhlmann, Former Zynga Chief Security Officer and Co-Founder of Cloud Security Alliance, Joins Endgame as Chief Technology Officer

Arlington, VA – November 6, 2013 – Endgame, Inc., a leading venture-backed provider of security solutions using data science to deliver actionable intelligence and visibility into the digital domain for government and commercial customers, today announced the appointment of Nils Puhlmann as its Chief Technology Officer. Mr. Puhlmann formerly served as Chief Security Officer of online gaming company Zynga. As CTO of Endgame, he will use his deep experience in the security industry to lead the company’s technical vision and strategy as it broadens its offering for enterprises.

Mr. Puhlmann has over 15 years of experience in executive-level security roles at software and web companies. As Chief Security Officer of Zynga from 2009-2012, he led the company’s converged security department, managing all security risks for the company and overseeing product and application security, security engineering and architecture, investigations and incident response, security intelligence and threat assessments, physical security, security compliance, and audit. He also chaired Zynga’s Security Risk Committee.

Mr. Puhlmann previously served in security leadership roles at Adobe, Electronic Arts, Mindjet, and Qualys. Additionally, he is a Co-Founder and Member of the Board of the Cloud Security Alliance, a community of 50,000 security professionals that promotes the use of best practices for providing security assurance within Cloud Computing.

“The opportunity to join Endgame and help drive the product and technical vision for the company at such an exciting time for data analytics and the broader security space is unmatched,” said Mr. Puhlmann. “I look forward to working with the Endgame team as it continues to do some of the most innovative, cutting-edge work in security data science.”

“Nils’s extensive security expertise, his passion for innovation, and his industry reputation will be invaluable as we continue to expand into the enterprise market,” said Endgame CEO Nate Fick. “I am thrilled to have Nils as a leader at Endgame during this next phase of the company’s growth.”

About Endgame

Endgame uses data science to bring clarity to the digital domain, allowing our federal and commercial partners to sense, discover, and act in real time. We provide data analytics driven by ease of use, scalability, speed, and effectiveness in order to solve our customers’ hardest problems.

Endgame was founded in 2008 and is backed by leading venture capital and private equity firms Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, Bessemer Venture Partners, Columbia Capital, Paladin Capital Group, and TechOperators. The company is headquartered in Arlington, VA with offices in Atlanta, GA, Columbia, MD, and San Antonio, TX.
