Application Migration Services

Migrating applications to newer technologies can give your business a leading edge while preserving your original business objectives, model and investment. For more than 17 years, Data Concepts has worked with clients to manage large-scale and project-specific migration initiatives to enhance their competitive position. We understand old and new technologies and the industry and business-specific drivers our clients face.

Our application migration Practice works closely with clients to recommend optimal technologies and the strategy that will drive their business success and provide a clear roadmap for seamless migration.

Enterprise Content Management



Strategy

We begin by analyzing your organization’s “as is” state, determine your desired content management state, and build a road map for your content management strategy.

Content Audit

In a content audit, we analyze the type, scope, format, domain, and create a comprehensive view of your organization’s content, which can be used for developing taxonomy and metadata standards.

Tool Evaluation

We assess your existing technology infrastructure. We analyze how and where specifics types of content are managed in the systems you own, such as:

  • Storage (databases)
  • Organization (taxonomy, metadata, tagging, CMS)
  • Access (portals, search)
We can help you evaluate any tools that you may need to purchase to help achieve the business goals of your company.
