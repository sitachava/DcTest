Heather Wiley

Heather Wiley is the VP of Human Resources at Endgame, responsible for human resources strategy and programs company wide. She is a strategic and innovative business partner with over 15 years of experience growing people practices at technology companies. Prior to joining Endgame, Heather was VP of Human Resources with a focus on Global Talent Acquisition, Employment Practices and Americas HR at Sourcefire, Inc., a publicly traded cyber security software company that was acquired by Cisco Systems, Inc. (CSCO) for $2.7 billion in 2013. Over the course of her 10-year career at Sourcefire she played a key role in building the company from a 79-person company to a global company with over 700 employees.
